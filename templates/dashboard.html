📋 Embed Chat Widget on Your Website

Copy and paste the following code into your website's HTML (before the closing </body> tag).

<script> window.CHATBOT_API_URL = '{{ request.url_root.rstrip('/') }}'; window.CHATBOT_NAME = 'Knowledge Bot'; window.CHATBOT_AVATAR = '🧠'; window.CHATBOT_COLOR = '#6C63FF'; window.CHATBOT_GREETING = 'Hello! Ask me anything about our documents.'; </script> <script src="{{ request.url_root }}widget.js"></script>
💬
Your visitors will see a chat bubble in the bottom-right corner. They must log in to use the chatbot.
